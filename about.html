about us

PILLARS DELIVERIES

Pillars Deliveries was founded in 2019. We have since grown to become the leading same-day delivery service on the Centre of Kenya, with a routed system covering Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru, and other Counties. We make well over a thousand deliveries a day. Our clientele include banks, online shops, medical laboratories, hospitals, government agencies, payroll services, manufacturing, schools and libraries.

With the recent need of deliveries, we have been challenged to further our commitment of excellence at Pillars Deliveries. As the role of same-day delivery services in today’s business world continues to evolve, we are changing with the times and will continue in our role as the leading same-day delivery service on the Centre of Kenya to strive to achieve service excellence.

JOSEPH NJUGUNA
Founder & CEO
